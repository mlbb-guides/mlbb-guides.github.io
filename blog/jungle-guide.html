1 Jungle Basics

Retribution Spell

Retribution is mandatory for junglers. It allows you to buy jungle items and deals bonus damage to jungle monsters. You MUST take this spell.

üî¥ Ice Retribution

Slows enemies. Best for assassins who need to stick to targets.

üîµ Flame Retribution

Deals damage and steals HP. Good for fighters and sustain junglers.

üü¢ Bloody Retribution

Reduces enemy damage output. Defensive option for diving into teams.

üîµ

Mana regen + cooldown reduction. Essential for mage junglers and mana-hungry heroes.

2 Optimal Pathing

üó∫Ô∏è Standard Opening Path (Blue Side)

  1. Blue Buff - Start here with roamer leash
  2. Small camp (next to blue)
  3. Lithowanderer - Clear for gold
  4. Red Buff - Take your second buff
  5. Look for gank - Check mid or gold lane

üîÑ After First Clear

Your rotation should follow this priority:

  1. Successful gank opportunity (enemy overextended)
  2. Turtle spawning soon (be there 15 seconds early)
  3. Your buff respawning (don't let enemy steal)
  4. Enemy buff available (invade if safe)
  5. Small camps and lane minions (tax sparingly)

üí° Farming Efficiency

Never walk past a jungle camp without clearing it. Always be farming something between ganks. Dead time = lost gold.

3 Ganking Guide

When to Gank

‚úì Good Gank Opportunities
  • Enemy pushed up past river (overextended)
  • Enemy used escape skill (dash/flicker on cooldown)
  • Your laner has CC to follow up
  • Enemy is low HP from trading
  • You have red buff for slow
‚úó Bad Gank Timing
  • Enemy under their tower
  • Enemy has full HP and escape ready
  • Enemy's teammates are nearby (check map!)
  • You're too low HP to fight
  • Big minion wave crashing into your laner

Lane Priority

Mid Lane HIGHEST PRIORITY

Short lane, easiest ganks, helps your mage rotate. Gank mid often.

Gold Lane HIGH PRIORITY

Marksmen are squishy and valuable. Getting your MM ahead wins late game.

EXP Lane SITUATIONAL

Fighters can usually handle themselves. Gank only if easy kill or tower dive.

4 Objective Control

Securing turtle and lord is YOUR job. This is what separates good junglers from great ones.

üê¢ Turtle Control

  • Spawns at 2:00 - Be there at 1:45 to set up
  • Track enemy jungler - If they show on map elsewhere, take turtle
  • Don't solo - Ask roamer to help. It's faster and safer.
  • Save retribution - Use it to secure the last hit
  • Ward the bush - Prevent steals with vision

üëë Lord Control

  • Spawns at 9:00 - Enhanced Lord at 12:00
  • Take after winning team fight - Don't force if enemies are up
  • Count enemies - Ideally, 2+ enemies should be dead
  • Zone for team - Sometimes your job is to protect the lord-takers
  • Lord steals win games - If enemy is on lord, position to steal

‚ö° Retribution Timing

Retribution deals damage based on your level. Know the exact numbers. Secure objectives at 20-30% HP with retribution. Don't use it early!

5 Best Jungle Heroes S35

S Tier - Top Meta Picks

Ling - Highest skill ceiling, wall-hopping assassin. Dominates when mastered.

Fanny - Cable god. Fastest jungle clear, insane mobility. Very hard to master.

Joy - Mobile mage jungler. Great burst and escape.

A Tier - Reliable Picks

Hayabusa - Safe assassin with great split push. Hard to catch.

Lancelot - Skill-based burst assassin. Immune frames on skills.

Roger - Fighter/Marksman hybrid. Versatile and consistent.

Karina - Reset assassin. Snowballs hard with kills.

B Tier - Solid Options

Aamon - Stealth assassin. Good for picks.

Alucard - Beginner-friendly, good sustain.

Saber - Point-and-click lockdown. Easy to execute.

Baxia - Tank jungler. Durable with good ganks.

6 Advanced Tips

üîÑ Invading Enemy Jungle

  • Invade when you spot enemy jungler on opposite side of map
  • Taking enemy buff denies them AND gives you double buff
  • Ward enemy jungle for information and invade opportunities
  • Don't invade if you're behind‚Äîyou'll just die

‚è±Ô∏è Tempo Control

  • If ahead: Force fights, invade, take objectives
  • If behind: Farm safely, look for picks, avoid 5v5
  • Track objective timers and plan around them
  • Your pace dictates the game‚Äîbe proactive, not reactive

üéØ Target Priority in Fights

  1. Enemy damage dealer (usually marksman or mage)
  2. Anyone out of position
  3. Low HP targets (secure kills, trigger resets)
  4. Avoid tanks unless they're the only target

üìä Tracking Cooldowns

Keep mental notes of enemy key abilities:

  • Flicker is 120 seconds. If used, you have a 2-min window.
  • Track enemy jungler's retribution for objective fights
  • Note when enemies use ultimates‚Äîthat's your engage window

üó°Ô∏è The Jungler's Creed

Farm fast. Gank smart. Secure objectives. Carry hard. The jungle belongs to those who dominate it.
