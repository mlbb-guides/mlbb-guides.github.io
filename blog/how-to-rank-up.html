How to Climb to Mythical Glory Fast

Proven strategies that top players use to rank up efficiently in Season 35

The Reality of Ranking Up

Let's be honest: ranking up in MLBB requires more than just mechanical skill. You need the right mindset, proper hero selection, good game sense, and sometimes, a bit of patience. This guide covers everything that separates players who are stuck from those who climb consistently.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: There's no magic trick. These strategies work, but they require consistent application over time.

1. Build the Right Hero Pool

Your hero pool determines your flexibility in draft. Here's what you need:

‚úÖ Ideal Hero Pool (8-12 heroes)

  • 2-3 heroes in your MAIN role (mastery level)
  • 2 heroes in a SECONDARY role (comfortable)
  • 1-2 flex picks that work in multiple roles
  • 1-2 meta picks you can play if needed

‚ùå Common Mistakes

  • Playing too many heroes (jack of all trades, master of none)
  • Only knowing one role (you'll be forced to play others)
  • Ignoring meta picks (some heroes are just stronger)
  • Picking mechanically hard heroes before mastering basics

2. Optimize Your Playing Schedule

When you play matters more than you think:

  • Best times: Late night (10PM-2AM) when casual players are asleep. More serious players online.
  • Avoid: Weekends during daytime, right after school hours, and first few days of new season.
  • Stop after 2 losses: Take a break. Tilt makes you play worse, and losing streaks are real.
  • Quality over quantity: 5 focused games > 15 tired games. Mental state affects performance.

3. Master the Draft Phase

Many games are won or lost before they even start. In ranked, draft is crucial:

1
Ban wisely

Ban heroes that counter your team's likely picks, or OP heroes that nobody on your team plays. Don't waste bans on heroes you can counter.

2
Show your pick early

Let teammates know what you want to play. Adjust if needed. Silent players cause double picks and bad compositions.

3
Counter-pick when possible

If you pick later, choose heroes that counter enemy picks. Check our counter guide for matchups.

4
Fill if necessary

Sometimes you need to adjust. A balanced team with a comfortable fill pick beats 5 carry players fighting for core.

4. Focus on Macro, Not Just Kills

The biggest difference between Legend and Mythic players is macro game sense:

Macro Priorities (in order)

  1. Don't die - Each death gives enemy gold/EXP and map control
  2. Farm efficiently - Never miss waves, take jungle when safe
  3. Take objectives - Convert kills into turrets, turtle, lord
  4. Control vision - Know where enemies are before fighting
  5. Then fight - Only with advantage (numbers, items, positioning)

5. Communication That Works

Use quick chat effectively. Don't type essays - use pings:

  • "Attack Lord/Turtle" - When you have advantage after a kill
  • "Retreat" - When outnumbered or enemies missing
  • "Gather" - Before important team fights
  • "Enemy has disappeared" - When your lane opponent leaves
  • Ping map locations - Where you see enemies or want to go

Never: Flame teammates, spam surrender, type during fights, or argue in chat. It never helps and often makes things worse.

6. Duo Queue Strategy

Playing with a reliable partner increases win rate significantly:

Best Duo Combinations:
  • Jungler + Roamer (coordinated ganks)
  • Mid + Roamer (mid lane dominance)
  • Gold + Roamer (protected carry)
Finding a Duo Partner:
  • Add players who performed well in your games
  • Look for complementary roles (not the same role)
  • Similar rank and play schedule
  • Positive attitude even when losing

7. Mental Game

Your mindset directly affects your performance:

  • Focus on yourself: You can't control teammates, only your own play. Get better and you'll climb.
  • Learn from losses: Watch replays. What could YOU have done differently? (Not what teammates should have done)
  • Mute toxic players: One button saves your mental and focus.
  • Accept variance: Some games are unwinnable. It happens to everyone. What matters is your average performance over 100 games.
  • Set realistic goals: Aim for one tier per week, not Mythical Glory in one day.

Rank-Specific Tips

Warrior ‚Üí Elite: Just learn the basics. Any hero works. Focus on not dying and farming.
Master ‚Üí Grandmaster: Learn one role properly. Understand basic objectives. Stop chasing kills.
Epic: Draft matters now. Learn counter-picks. Focus on macro play. This is where most players get stuck.
Legend: Master 2-3 meta heroes. Improve team fight positioning. Study pro player patterns.
Mythic ‚Üí Mythical Glory: Every small advantage counts. Perfect your mechanics. Play consistently over many games.
