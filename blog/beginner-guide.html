Complete Beginner's Guide to MLBB

Everything new players need to know to start winning in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

What is Mobile Legends?

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang (MLBB) is a 5v5 multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed by Moonton. Two teams of five players compete to destroy the enemy's base while defending their own. The game features over 120 unique heroes, each with special abilities and roles.

Matches typically last 10-20 minutes, making it perfect for mobile gaming. The game is free-to-play with optional cosmetic purchases.

Understanding the Map

The map consists of three lanes (Gold Lane, Mid Lane, and EXP Lane), a jungle area between lanes, and each team's base. Here's what you need to know:

  • Gold Lane (Bottom): Gives bonus gold from minions. Perfect for Marksmen who need items to deal damage.
  • Mid Lane (Center): The shortest lane. Usually for Mages who can rotate quickly to help other lanes.
  • EXP Lane (Top): Gives bonus experience from minions. Ideal for Fighters who scale with levels.
  • Jungle: Contains monsters that give gold, EXP, and buffs. The Jungler farms here.

Key Objectives

Objectives win games. Learning when to take them is crucial:

  • Turrets: Destroy enemy turrets to open up the map and get closer to their base. Each turret gives gold to your team.
  • Turtle (Spawns at 2:00): Gives gold and EXP to your team. Prioritize when you have lane advantage.
  • Lord (Spawns at 9:00): A powerful ally that pushes lanes. Take it after winning a team fight or when enemies are dead.
  • Base: Destroy the enemy base to win. Don't get distracted by kills - objectives matter more!

Essential Tips for New Players

1
Farm, don't fight early

Focus on killing minions and jungle monsters. Gold and EXP from farming is more reliable than risky fights.

2
Check the minimap constantly

Glance at your minimap every few seconds. If enemies are missing, play safe - they might be coming to gank you.

3
Don't chase kills

Chasing enemies into fog of war gets you killed. Take what you can safely and push objectives instead.

4
Master 2-3 heroes per role

Don't try to learn every hero at once. Pick easy heroes first (Layla, Tigreal, Miya) and master them.

5
Stay with your team late game

After 10 minutes, group up. Getting caught alone can cost you the game. Move as 4-5 when pushing.
