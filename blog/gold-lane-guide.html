Gold Lane Marksman Guide

Master farming, positioning, and late-game carry potential

Season 35 ‚Ä¢ Updated January 2025 ‚Ä¢ 11 min read

üèπ The Late Game Insurance

Gold laners are the team's insurance policy. You're weak early, vulnerable mid-game, but if you farm well and survive, you become the ultimate damage dealer. Your job is to not die and hit your power spikes.

1 Gold Lane Basics

üìç Lane Location

  • Blue side: Gold lane is BOTTOM
  • Red side: Gold lane is TOP
  • Gold lane gives +25% gold from minions
  • This extra gold is why MM goes here‚Äîfaster item spikes

üìã Core Responsibilities

  1. Farm efficiently - Last hit every minion
  2. Stay alive - A dead MM farms nothing
  3. Hit power spikes - First 2 core items ASAP
  4. Push turret when safe - Take gold lane tower
  5. Deal damage late game - You're the DPS in team fights

2 Farming Efficiency

‚ö° Last Hitting

  • Every missed minion = 50-100 gold lost
  • 10 missed minions = almost a full item component
  • Practice last hitting in custom games
  • Use skills to secure contested last hits

üåä Wave Management

  • Freeze near tower: When scared or behind. Safe farming.
  • Slow push: Build a big wave before rotating.
  • Fast push: When enemy roams, shove and take plates.
  • Never let waves crash into tower: You lose gold.

üå≥ Jungle Farm

  • Take nearby small camps between waves
  • DON'T take jungler's buffs (unless they offer)
  • Invade enemy small camps when safe
  • Lithowanderer is free gold‚Äîtake it

üí∞ Gold Benchmarks

Aim for these gold numbers to stay on pace:

  • 5 min: 2,500+ gold (first core item)
  • 10 min: 5,000+ gold (two core items)
  • 15 min: 8,000+ gold (three items)

3 Positioning & Kiting

Positioning is 80% of playing marksman. Good positioning means you deal damage without dying.

üìç Positioning Rules

  1. Stay behind your frontline - ALWAYS. Tanks exist for a reason.
  2. Maintain max attack range - Hit from as far as possible.
  3. Have an escape route - Know where you'll run if dove.
  4. Don't chase - Let enemies come to you.
  5. Hit closest target - Don't tunnel on backline.

üèÉ Kiting Technique

Kiting = attacking while moving backward. Essential for MM.

  1. Attack enemy
  2. Immediately move backward during attack animation
  3. Attack again when animation resets
  4. Repeat while maintaining distance

Pro tip: Use attack joystick + move joystick together. Practice in custom games.

4 Team Fight Role

‚öîÔ∏è Fight Entry Timing

TOO EARLY

Entering first = instant death. Let tanks engage.

PERFECT TIMING

Enter after enemy uses key abilities. Start DPSing from safe distance.

TOO LATE

Waiting too long = team dies before you help. Find the balance.

üéØ Target Priority

  1. Whoever is closest AND safe to hit - Your main job
  2. Enemy diving you - Self-defense priority
  3. Low HP targets - Secure kills for reset heroes
  4. Enemy carries - Only if safe to reach

Key insight: Hitting the enemy tank is FINE if that's who you can safely hit. Dead DPS = 0 damage.

6 Common Mistakes

‚ùå "I need to join every fight"

If you're underfarmed, it's better to push a lane. Being 2 levels down = useless in fights.

‚ùå Face-checking bushes

Let tanks check bushes. One wrong bush = death = 30+ seconds no farming.

‚ùå Chasing kills

Chasing = walking into enemy territory = getting collapsed on = death. Don't.

‚ùå Building wrong items

Don't build pure damage. Wind of Nature and Immortality save lives.

‚ùå Farming alone late game

After 12 minutes, always have a teammate nearby. Getting picked = losing Lord.

üèπ The Marksman Mantra

Stay alive. Farm consistently. Position correctly. Let items do the talking. A late-game MM with full build is the scariest thing in MLBB.
