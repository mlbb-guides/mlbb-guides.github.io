Complete Roamer Guide

Master vision, rotations, and team support to control the game

1 Roamer Basics

The roamer is the team's utility player. You provide vision, protection, initiation, and support‚Äîall while taking minimal gold from the map.

üìã Core Responsibilities

  • Help jungler clear - Leash first buff to speed up clear
  • Provide vision - Check bushes, ward key areas
  • Rotate to create advantages - Turn 1v1s into 2v1s
  • Protect carries - Peel for MM and mage in fights
  • Initiate fights - When you have the advantage
  • Secure/contest objectives - Be at every turtle and lord

‚ö†Ô∏è Golden Rule

NEVER take minions or jungle camps unless absolutely necessary. Roaming boots penalize you for last-hitting. Your gold comes from assists and roam item passive.

2 Roaming Boots

Your first item is always roaming boots. Choose based on team needs:

üü¢ Encourage (Courage Boots)

Effect: Buffs nearby allies with attack/defense when abilities hit enemies.

Best for: Aggressive tanks with frequent skill usage (Tigreal, Atlas, Khufra)

üü£ Conceal (Shadow Boots)

Effect: Makes you and nearby ally invisible while moving in bushes.

Best for: Setting up ganks and surprise engages. Great with jungler.

üîµ Favor (Healing Boots)

Effect: Heals and speeds up nearby low-HP allies.

Best for: Protecting squishy carries. Good for support-style roamers.

üî¥ Dire Hit (Damage Boots)

Effect: Increases damage dealt to enemies you mark.

Best for: Burst-oriented teams. Helps delete priority targets.

3 Vision Control

Vision is information, and information wins games. You need to check bushes constantly and read the map.

üëÅÔ∏è Key Vision Points

  • River bushes - Most common gank paths
  • Enemy jungle entrances - Track their jungler
  • Turtle/Lord pit - Essential for objective control
  • Behind turrets - Spot dive attempts
  • Mid lane bushes - Protect your mage's rotations

üîç Vision Habits

‚úì Walk into bushes first‚Äînever let carries face-check
‚úì If 2+ enemies are missing, ping danger and check bushes
‚úì Track enemy cooldowns (especially flicker and key ultimates)
‚úì Use hero skill indicators to check bushes from distance

4 Rotation Timing

‚è±Ô∏è Early Game (0:00 - 4:00)

  1. Help jungler clear first buff (leash)
  2. Move to mid lane, check bushes
  3. Rotate with jungler for ganks
  4. Be at turtle 15 seconds before spawn (1:45)
  5. After turtle, help pressure gold lane

üîÑ Mid Game (4:00 - 10:00)

  • Follow your jungler - You're their bodyguard
  • Group for objectives - Never let turtle/lord be a 50-50
  • Rotate to winning lanes - Snowball advantages
  • Protect struggling lanes - Prevent enemy from snowballing

üë• Late Game (10:00+)

  • Stay grouped - Getting picked loses games
  • Protect carries - Your MM is priority #1
  • Initiate carefully - Bad engage = lost game
  • Contest Lord - This objective often decides games

5 Team Fight Role

‚öîÔ∏è Engage Tanks (Tigreal, Atlas, Khufra)

  • Look for multi-hero ultimates
  • Flicker + Ultimate is your bread and butter
  • Wait for enemies to group before engaging
  • Communicate "I go" before diving in

üõ°Ô∏è Peel Tanks (Lolita, Belerick, Hylos)

  • Stay near your damage dealers
  • Block dashes and projectiles
  • CC anyone who dives your backline
  • Use body-blocking to absorb skill shots

‚ú® Support Heroes (Rafaela, Estes, Angela)

  • Position behind frontline
  • Focus heals on whoever is taking damage
  • Use CC to interrupt key enemy abilities
  • Save ultimate for crucial moments (not first second of fight)

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes

  • Engaging without team - Check your team is ready
  • Chasing kills - Your job is protect, not kill
  • Using all CC at once - Chain CC, don't stack it
  • Dying first - You're useless if dead early

6 Best Roamer Heroes S35

S Tier - Top Meta Picks

Atlas - Best engage ultimate in the game. Flicker + Ult wins fights.

Khufra - Anti-dash tank. Counters mobile meta heroes.

Chou - High skill cap. Incredible picks and peel.

A Tier - Reliable Picks

Tigreal - Beginner-friendly. Consistent engage.

Franco - Hook god. Game-changing picks.

Rafaela - Excellent sustain and speed boost.

Estes - Pure healer. Great vs poke comps.

B Tier - Situational

Lolita - Excellent vs projectile heroes (MM, mages).

Minotaur - Rage mode is devastating when timed well.

Belerick - Counter to basic attack heroes.

üõ°Ô∏è The Roamer's Pride

You may not get the kills. You may not get the glory. But your team wins because of you. That's the true measure of a great roamer.
