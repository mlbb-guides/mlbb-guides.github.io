Draft Picking & Ban Strategy

Win games before they even start with smart drafting

Season 35 ‚Ä¢ Updated January 2025 ‚Ä¢ 10 min read

üìã The Hidden Game

Many games are decided in draft before minions spawn. A team with better composition, counters to enemy picks, and synergy has a massive advantage. Master the draft, and you'll win more games without improving your mechanics.

1 Banning Strategy

üö´ Ban Categories

Tier 1: OP Bans

Current broken heroes that dominate the meta. Check tier list for S+ tier.

Tier 2: Comfort Bans

Heroes your team doesn't know how to play against. No shame in this.

Tier 3: Target Bans

If you see enemy hover a one-trick hero, ban it. Remove their carry.

Tier 4: Comp Denial

Ban heroes that counter YOUR team's plan or enable enemy's strategy.

2 Picking Order Strategy

üìã Pick Order Principles

First Pick (1st)

Take contested meta picks or flex picks. Risk: enemy can counter you.

Second Pick (2nd-3rd)

React to enemy first pick with counters. Lock in core roles.

Fourth Pick (4th)

Counter-pick enemy carries. Fill missing roles.

Last Pick (5th)

Hard counter opportunity. Enemy can't react. Save for carries if possible.

üí° Strategic Insight

Don't reveal your carry picks early. If you're playing MM or Mage, let teammates pick first. Last-pick your carry to avoid being countered.

3 Counter-Picking

Know who counters who. This knowledge wins drafts.

üó°Ô∏è Countering Assassins

  • Lolita - Blocks projectiles
  • Phoveus - Punishes dash-heavy heroes

üèπ Countering Marksmen

  • Saber - Point-and-click lockdown

üîÆ Countering Mages

  • Athena's Shield - Item counter for magic burst
  • Radiant Armor - Reduces sustained magic damage
  • Dive assassins - Delete mages before they cast

üõ°Ô∏è Countering Tanks

4 Team Composition

‚úÖ Balanced Composition Checklist

  • ‚òê Frontline - At least 1 tank or fighter who can absorb damage
  • ‚òê Engage - Someone who can start fights (tank or assassin)
  • ‚òê Sustained DPS - Usually marksman for late game damage
  • ‚òê Burst damage - Mage or assassin for quick kills
  • ‚òê Mixed damage - Both physical AND magic damage
  • ‚òê CC - Stuns, knock-ups, slows to control fights

Early Game Comp

Strong laners + aggressive jungler. Win lanes, take objectives, end fast. Falls off late.

Example: Chou, Hayabusa, Pharsa, Beatrix, Tigreal

Late Game Comp

Hyper carries + scaling heroes. Survive early, dominate late. Need good defense.

Example: Estes, Roger, Cecilion, Claude, Atlas

Dive Comp

Multiple divers who jump backline together. Overwhelm enemy carries.

Example: Chou, Ling, Selena, Wanwan, Khufra

Poke Comp

Long-range heroes who whittle down enemies before fights. Avoid all-ins.

Example: Lolita, Yve, Layla, Chang'e, Pharsa

5 Flex Picks

Flex picks can be played in multiple roles, hiding your strategy from the enemy.

Chou - EXP lane, Roamer, Jungler
Valentina - Mid lane, Jungler, EXP lane
Selena - Mid lane, Roamer, Jungler
Benedetta - EXP lane, Jungler, Mid lane
Mathilda - Roamer, Mid lane
Silvanna - EXP lane, Jungler, Mid lane

üí° Flex Pick Strategy

Pick flex heroes early. Enemy won't know where they're going. Finalize roles in last picks based on enemy composition.

6 Team Communication

üí¨ Draft Phase Communication

  • Show your hero preference - Hover what you want to play
  • Call your role early - "I jungle" / "I gold"
  • Suggest bans - "Ban Ling" / "Ban their hover"
  • Offer to swap picks - Pick for teammate to prevent counter
  • Discuss composition - "We need tank" / "All physical damage"

‚úÖ Good Draft Behavior

  • Adjust your pick for team needs
  • Fill roles if no one else will
  • Listen to teammate suggestions
  • Stay positive even with bad draft

‚ùå Bad Draft Behavior

  • Instalocking without communication
  • Refusing to play any other role
  • Flaming teammates for picks
  • Giving up before game starts

üìã The Draft Master's Truth

A better draft doesn't guarantee victory, but it creates advantages. Stack enough small advantages in draft, and you'll find yourself winning games that felt impossible before.
