How often is the tier list updated?

We update our tier list after every major patch, typically within 24-48 hours. We also make adjustments based on meta shifts observed in professional tournaments and high-rank matches.

Are the builds based on real data?

Yes! Our builds are based on data analysis from high-rank matches (Legend to Mythical Glory), professional player preferences, and extensive testing. We prioritize builds that consistently perform well across different matchups.

Can I suggest a hero or build?

Absolutely! Join our Discord community or send us an email with your suggestions. We're always looking to improve and expand our content based on community feedback.

Is MLBB Pro affiliated with Moonton?

No, MLBB Pro Guides is an independent fan-made resource. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Moonton or Mobile Legends: Bang Bang in any official capacity.
