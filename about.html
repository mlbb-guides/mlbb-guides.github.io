üìä Our Methodology

How We Create Builds

Every hero build on our site goes through a rigorous testing process before publication:

1

Data Collection: We analyze 100,000+ match replays from Legend to Mythic Glory ranks using custom data extraction tools. This gives us statistically significant sample sizes for each hero.

2

Pro Tournament Analysis: We watch MPL (Mobile Legends: Bang Bang Professional League), ESL, and regional tournaments to identify pro player preferences and trending strategies. This gives us insight into what works at the highest level of play.

3

In-Game Testing: Each build is tested in 20+ ranked games across different skill ranges (Epic, Legend, Mythic). We record win rates, power spike timings, and matchup effectiveness to verify the build performs as expected.

4

Community Feedback: We incorporate feedback from our Discord community of 5,000+ players. If a build consistently underperforms in real matches, we revise or remove it.

5

Publication Criteria: Builds are only published when they maintain a 52%+ win rate in our testing and align with current meta. This ensures we only recommend what actually works.
