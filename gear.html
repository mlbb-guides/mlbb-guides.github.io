MLBB Hardware Requirements Guide

üì± What Phone Specs Do You Need for MLBB?

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang runs well on most modern smartphones, but for competitive ranked play, you'll want specific specs. The game is optimized to run at 60 FPS on mid-range devices, but high-end devices can push 120+ FPS with Ultra graphics. For smooth gameplay, aim for at least 4GB RAM, a Snapdragon 600-series or Dimensity 700-series processor, and 60Hz display. For competitive play (Legend rank and above), we recommend 8GB+ RAM, Snapdragon 8-series or Dimensity 8000+, and a 120Hz+ display for faster reaction times.

üéÆ Do Gaming Triggers Actually Help?

Yes, gaming triggers provide a real advantage for certain heroes. Heroes like Fanny, Gusion, and Chou require precise timing and rapid skill execution. Physical triggers allow 4-6 finger gameplay, letting you aim while casting skills simultaneously. This is especially important for skill-shot heavy heroes where you need to move while aiming. Many professional players and top streamers use triggers or dedicated gaming phones with built-in shoulder buttons.

‚ùÑÔ∏è Why Does Phone Cooling Matter?

Extended gaming sessions cause phones to heat up, leading to "thermal throttling" where the processor slows down to prevent overheating. This directly impacts your FPS, causing frame drops during crucial team fights. A cooling fan or cooling clip can reduce device temperature by 10-30¬∞C, maintaining consistent performance throughout long ranked sessions. This is especially important in hot climates or when playing multiple games back-to-back.

üéß Audio Settings Tips

Good audio helps you hear important sound cues like enemy skill sounds, recall audio, and Lord/Turtle spawns. For the best experience, use earbuds with low latency (under 100ms) - wired is best for zero delay. In MLBB settings, turn off background music and keep sound effects at 80-100%. This helps you focus on game sounds that indicate enemy positions and skill usage.
