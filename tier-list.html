How We Rank Heroes

Our tier list is based on multiple factors including win rate data from high-rank matches (Legend to Mythical Glory), professional tournament meta, and patch note analysis. We update this list after every major patch to ensure accuracy.

Tier Explanations

  • S+ Tier: Extremely overpowered. Must ban or first pick. These heroes are broken in the current meta.
  • S Tier: Overpowered. Ban or pick priority. These heroes dominate the current meta.
  • A Tier: Very strong. Reliable picks that can carry games consistently.
  • B Tier: Good. Situationally strong or require skill to maximize potential.
  • C Tier: Weak in current meta. Needs buffs or easily countered by popular picks.

Season 35 Meta Overview

The current meta favors hyper-aggressive junglers and roaming supports. With the new Sky Piercer item, assassins like Hayabusa and Saber have become even more lethal. Tank Chou remains a top pick in professional play, while Zhuxin and Suyou are the newest heroes making waves in ranked matches.
