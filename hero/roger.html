üí° Pro Tips & Gameplay Guide

Roger is currently the best jungler in the game. In human form, poke enemies and slow them with S1 while gaining attack speed from S2. When enemies drop below 40% HP, transform into wolf form to execute them with enhanced damage. The transformation itself deals damage, so use it as part of your burst combo. Great Dragon Spear reduces your ultimate cooldown, allowing more frequent transformations.

Quick Tip: Human form = range poke. Wolf form = execute low HP. Ult at 40% enemy HP for execute. Top tier jungler.
