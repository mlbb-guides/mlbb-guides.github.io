üí° Pro Tips & Gameplay Guide

Hayabusa is the king of isolated picks. Always look for enemies farming alone or split from their team. Your ultimate deals FULL damage to a single target but is split when multiple enemies are present. Use S2 to create an escape shadow before engaging. The Sky Piercer execute passive guarantees kills on low HP targets. Master the S1>S2>Ult>S2 (teleport back) combo for safe assassinations.

Quick Tip: Sky Piercer executes below 6-12% HP. Ult ISOLATED targets for max damage. Always have shadow for escape.
